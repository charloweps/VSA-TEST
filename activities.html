mental health talks

Our members' mental health wellbeing is a priority to us.

Veterinary school is not a walk in the park for our members. The tight schedules and lots of work load can be mentally depressing and stressful. Therefore, we as the VSA have taken it as our personal mandate to ensure the mental well being of our members. We do this by holding mental health talk sessions with our members and having guest speakers who ge to share their knowledge and insights about mental health and how to cope with the issues that come with it.

vsa sports

Apart from books, we fancy sports too.

Sometimes our bodies need a break from the usual 8 to 5 studying routine and what better way to recharge than by engaging in sporting activities. The VSA organizes sporting events each semester where our members get to engage in sporting activities such as football, board games, volleyball among others. Through this, our members not only get to explore their different sporting skills and talents but also get a chance to compete for different awards.
